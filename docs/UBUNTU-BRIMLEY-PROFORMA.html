01 — The verdict

It works — because of the forgivable loan.

On a blended affordable/market rent roll the building is worth about $10,135,037 — below its $12.0M cost. The deal pencils because the Build Canada Homes forgivable loan, the City contribution, and the discounted land equity fund the $1,852,733 gap that rents alone can't finance.

07 — What breaks it

Rents and the rate.

DSCR is the binding constraint. With 30% of units held at 80% of median market rent, coverage is most sensitive to the achievable blended market rent and the locked MLI Select rate.
